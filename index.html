Columbia

Columbia is an educational organization that provides online programs for schools.

What We Do

We offer structured online courses that include recorded lectures, readings, and assignments designed to support teachers and schools.

Student Surveys

As part of our programs, students complete confidential surveys related to school climate and bullying. The results help teachers understand patterns and improve the learning environment.

For Teachers

Teachers receive clear, easy-to-understand results, including summaries and visual insights, to support informed decision-making.
